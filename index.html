    Portfolio

    My projects organized by year and medium.
    Digital Media
    Digital artworks made in HeavyPaint (IOS) and Procreate(IOS).
    3D Models
    A collection of my 3D models and their environments. Some models were sculpted following a traditional intuitive process, others are low poly. Some are rigged while others are used as references for my 2D pieces. Sculpted in Nomad (IOS) or Blender, rigged and developed in Blender.
    Traditional Artwork
    Artworks done using watercolor. I usually use a wet-on-wet technique for my pieces. When I use traditional media, I focus on displaying a good understanding of rendering, proportions, and the unique features of my chosen medium.
    Experiments and Studies
    A collection of exploratory pieces. I primarily do studies in order to learn how to orient models in 3D spaces (without the aid of 3D modelling software), and how to sketch in perspective. Other pieces showcase style development.

    About Me

    My name is Michelle. I'm a second year computer science student. I draw and paint in my spare time.
